    AI Excellence Center—Central Europe

    Explore how the HPE AI Excellence Center helps you unlock real business value from AI—going beyond the hype to discover transformative use cases that deliver measurable ROI. Dive into cutting-edge innovation that redefines business models and accelerates your journey from insight to impact.

    What is the HPE AI Excellence Center?

    In a world where AI is everywhere, separating noise from real impact can be a challenge. The HPE AI Excellence Center is your trusted partner to cut through the hype and focus on what truly matters: unlocking measurable business value. We bring together deep domain expertise, advanced technology, and a proven methodology to help you identify high-impact AI use cases tailored to your industry and unique business challenges.

    Whether you're exploring AI for the first time or scaling existing initiatives, our experts work with you to co-create solutions that drive positive ROI, streamline operations, and open new revenue streams. From idea to deployment, we help you build explainable, ethical, and scalable AI that delivers tangible outcomes—not just experiments.

    Step inside the HPE AI Excellence Center and experience how applied AI can reshape your business model, enhance decision-making, and create lasting competitive advantage.

    Feel free to contact us under aiec-ce@hpe.com.

    Explore our core topics

    From building trustworthy AI to leveraging token economies and benchmarking technologies—discover how our expert-driven focus areas empower your organization to turn AI into real business impact.

    HPE AI Factory Portfolio

    HPE’s AI Factory Portfolio offers three tailored platforms: Turnkey AI Factory for enterprise-ready private cloud AI, AI Factory at Scale for model builders needing scalable, multi-tenant solutions, and Sovereign AI Factory for secure, compliant AI within national borders—each supporting the full AI lifecycle with integrated infrastructure, software, and services.

    AI Partner Ecosystem

    The HPE AI Partner Ecosystem brings together ISVs, consultants, startups, and HPE experts to co-develop AI solutions. By aligning on platforms, infrastructure, and use-case-driven innovation, this ecosystem accelerates AI project execution, fosters co-innovation, and drives scalable growth across industries. It is the European arm of HPE’s Global Unleash AI Program, empowering partners to deliver responsible, production-grade AI.

    Trustworthy AI

    Trust is the foundation of successful AI adoption.

    At the HPE AI Excellence Center, we help you design and deploy AI systems that are explainable, fair, and secure.

    From ethical risk assessments to bias mitigation strategies, we ensure your AI operates within regulatory frameworks and societal expectations—building confidence across stakeholders.

    Token economics

    Most modern AI systems operate on token-based pricing models, where costs scale with usage and limited predictability. What often seems affordable at first glance can quickly become a major expense. We help you understand the true cost dynamics of tokenized AI usage. Through precise customer-focused analysis, we identify the exact break-even point for your AI investments, optimize your AI operating model to minimize token consumption, and maximize your AI-related costs control—without sacrificing on performance.

    Benchmarking tests

    Not all AI solutions are created equal.

    Our comprehensive benchmarking services provide you with clear, objective evaluations of AI performance across different models, frameworks, and hardware. This allows you to make data-driven choices that match your business goals—whether you prioritize accuracy, speed, energy efficiency, or scalability.

    AI Workshops for executives & AI leaders

    AI isn’t just a technology shift—it’s a leadership challenge.

    Our tailored workshops are designed to empower executives and people managers with the strategic understanding and practical tools they need to lead AI-driven transformation.

    We translate complex topics into actionable insights, fostering a culture that embraces innovation and change.
